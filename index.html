Welcome to your next out of this world vacation

Welcome to Star Tours! Our world class operating system will allow you to search for planets in the galaxy that you may want to visit for your next vacation destination! Simply search for a planets name in the search bar. Results will appear based on matching planets. Information will be displayed on the Planets such as Climate, Terrain, Population size, and Percentage of water on the surface. Once you know where you would like to fly to, click on the Add Planet button, and it will be added to the Itinerary field of the webpage! Now you are all set for your next vacation amongst the stars! If you accidentally booked an incorrect flight, Don't worry, you can always click on the Remove Planets button in the Itinerary field to clear up your schedule!
